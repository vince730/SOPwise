What's inside

What's inside your training package

Every SOP you upload goes through a four-stage pipeline. What comes back is not a reformatted document. It is a training package built around what a worker actually does.

  • Plain-language task steps Reorganised around real actions, not document sections. "Responding to a Chemical Spill" instead of "Section 16: Chemical Spills and Leaks."
  • Critical-mistake checklist The errors that matter most, pulled from the SOP and flagged so a new starter knows what not to get wrong on day one.
  • Scenario questions Judgment-testing situations based on the SOP content. Not multiple-choice filler. Questions that check whether someone can actually apply what they have read.
  • Supervisor sign-off sheet A structured checklist your team leads can use to confirm competency. Audit-ready from the moment it is printed.
  • Verification against the original The final stage checks the output against your source document so nothing critical gets lost in translation.
